Our Mission

The mission of Grace Church is to glorify God by obeying the Great Commandment (Matthew 22: 36-40) and the Great Commission (Matthew 28: 18-20). The process is designed to pro- duce disciples of Jesus Christ which involves at least the following four categories.

1. Worship -Celebrating

"To love God with all your heart..."

Matt. 22: 36-40

2. Ministry-(Caring)

"Love your neighbor as yourself..."

Matt. 22: 36-40

3. Evangelism-(Cultivating)

"Go... and make disciples..."

Matt. 28:18-20

4. Discipleship-(Communicating)

"Teach them to observe..."

Matt. 28:18-20

About Us

We are a group of individuals who are broken, hurt, and im- perfect. However, the love of God has changed us with the life, death, and resurrection of His Son Jesus Christ. We are contin- ually being transformed with the power of the Holy Spirit and we invite you to join us in the quest to allow God to perfect us little by little and day by day.

Our Pastor

Pastor Juan Ayala was raised in Houston’s second ward in the east side of town. God saved him at the age of 18 while attend- ing a revival at a local church. In 2001, he was called into the pastoral ministry where he has now served for more than 18 years. Since then he has received a bachelor’s degree from the College of Biblical Studies (2009) and his master’s degree from Dallas Theological Seminary (2014). In January of 2016 he became senior pastor at Grace Church where he has been ministering for the last four years. Pastor Juan Ayala is married to his beautiful wife Lucia for more than 25 years and they have two wonderful daughters Maribel and Monica.

Doctrinal Statement

While we adhere to a great number of biblical doctrines here are
what we believe are the seven core doctrines of our faith.

The authority and inerrancy of Scripture

The Trinity

Christ’s full deity and humanity

The spiritual lostness of the human race

Christ’s substitutionary atonement and bodily resurrection

Salvation by grace alone through faith alone in Christ alone

The physical return of Christ
